No animation without narrative purpose. The typewriter reveal IS the product experience — it's the moment the AI clue arrives. The pulse on the active pin says "you are here." Everything else is a transition, not a show.

07 / Decisions

Why These Choices

Decision Rationale Type
Fraunces serif for display Every competitor uses sans-serif for everything. A serif says "artifact, document, mission" not "app button." Immediate category distinction. Risk
Amber/gold primary accent Category uses teal/orange-red/green. Gold = reward, cantina warmth, achievement. Nobody else occupies this space. Requires careful pairing to avoid "cheap" feel. Risk
JetBrains Mono for clue text Clues are AI-generated transmissions, not SMS. The monospace + typewriter animation is the moment the product earns its theme. Keep clues short. Risk
Dark map + styled pins Already baked into the stack (Google Maps dark style). Users expect a map. Zero learning curve. Safe
Card list with lock states Category baseline. Users immediately understand locked/unlocked/complete without instructions. Safe
Large QR scan CTA Outdoor use, bright sun, sweaty hands. The scan button is the primary action — it gets all the real estate. Safe
